How the booking experience works

The redesigned journey is built around donor-driven booking, recommendation-led guidance, date and time capture, availability validation, progressive alternatives, and final confirmation.

1

Identify donor

Optional identification retrieves the donor context needed to support the booking journey.

2

Recommend a day

For authenticated donors, the experience can start with a recommended date and compensation.

3

Capture date and time

The donor provides a preferred day and time naturally, with clarification only when needed.

4

Confirm or offer alternatives

The system validates availability and either confirms the slot or proposes nearby options.

What this demo showcases

The content below is intentionally centered on appointments, not eligibility, so the page matches the redesign scope and the voice demo behavior.

📅

Book using natural date and time

Donors can say a full request, only a day, only a time, or a partial answer.

The experience guides the conversation step by step until the slot is clear.

💰

Smart compensation recommendation

The booking flow may proactively recommend a donation day together with the associated compensation.

If accepted, the donor skips directly to time selection for that recommended day.

🕒

Slot-based appointment handling

Appointments follow fixed slot increments, with different granularity depending on donor type.

Existing donors use 15-minute increments, while new donors use 30-minute increments.

🔄

Alternatives when the requested time is unavailable

If the requested slot cannot be offered, the donor is guided through nearby options, later times, or a different day.

The flow avoids dead ends and keeps the conversation short and manageable.

🏥

Center-aware appointment logic

Booking respects center opening days, operating hours, and local time for the selected location.

Appointments are never proposed outside valid center schedules.

✅

Final confirmation

Once a valid slot is accepted, the appointment is created and confirmed with a final date and time.

The donor receives a clear end-state, suitable for a voice-first journey.
