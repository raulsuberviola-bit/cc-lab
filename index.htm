Are you 18-69 years old?

To become a plasma donor, you must be able to prove you are between the ages of 18 and 69 years old.

⚖️

Do you weigh over 110 lbs. / 50kg?

If you weigh less than 110 lbs, your body may not be able to tolerate the donation process as well as someone who weighs more.

🪪

Can you provide a valid photo I.D., proof of Social Security number or Border Crossing Card, and proof of your current address?

These are the documents we check at your first visit before continuing with the donation process. We’re required to verify your identity at every visit.

If you’re not a US citizen, you can provide your Border Crossing Card (B1/B2 VISA/BBC).

💙

Are you in good overall health?

Meaning, are you active, try to eat well-balanced meals, and generally get a good night’s rest?

❔

Did you know?

You can donate plasma twice within a week, as long as your donations are two days apart from each other.
